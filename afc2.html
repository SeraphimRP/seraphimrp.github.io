What is it?

AFC is our annual competition to raise funds for charity.

The competition begins as of this posting. The competition ends December 3rd, 11:59pm Eastern Standard Time. Winners will be announced the following day.

What do I win?

The top 5 donors will receive a permanent vanity role with their placement.

The top 2 will additionally receive one month of Discord Nitro.

Finally, the top donor will additionally receive a $100 gift certificate to St. Vladimir's Seminary Press or Ancient Faith Store, their choice.

What's the catch?

For you personally, nothing. However:
  • Donations must be given in the form of currency to an approved charity (see below).
  • AmazonSmile donations do not apply.
  • All donations must be processed and receipts sent to srp#0001's DMs by competition end.
  • Multiple donations are allowed and are stackable.
  • Do not boast your donation publicly.
  • Purchasing an item from the store or gift shop of any charity does not count as a donation.
